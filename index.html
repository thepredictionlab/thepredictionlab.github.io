What we do

Our mission is to advance complex systems analysis, improving prediction and control. We solve hard problems using math, machines and creativity.

Consume Data

Data is king, but often hard to work with. We use techniques from statistics, applied mathematics, machine learning and AI to find the signal hidden within the noise.

Predict and Control

We specialize in predicting and controling outcomes in wicked systems --those where change can come out of nowhere. Our work is broad, inlcuding ecosystems, competitive team (e)sports and financial markets.

About

A brief history of us.

  • 2017

    Our Beginnings

    The Prediction Lab (TPL) originated through a series of advanced research projects at Oregon State University developing new methods for analyzing data from complex systems; anything from financial markets to fish schools!

  • 2018

    TPL starts

    Knowing that our basic scientific research could add value to many areas, TPL was formed and immediately started working on solving problems.

  • 2019

    Harmful algal bloom predictions

    Our first job was with the City of Salem predicting harmful algal blooms in Detroit Lake, the source of drinking water in the region. A super challenging but rewarding project.

  • Present

    Phase Two Expansion

    In addition to maintaining expertise in and passion for environmental data problems, TPL is now pushing into two new areas: 1) sports data analytics and 2) financial data analysis.
